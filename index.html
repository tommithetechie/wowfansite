Tomes of Knowledge

Prince Arthas Menethil, once the paladin champion of Lordaeron, journeyed to the frozen wastes of Northrend seeking the cursed runeblade, Frostmourne. He claimed the weapon to save his people, but instead, it claimed his soul. Returning home as a Death Knight, he murdered his father and shattered his own kingdom, paving the way for the Scourge.

Ascending the Frozen Throne, Arthas shattered the ice encasing the spirit of the orc shaman Ner'zhul. Donning the Helm of Domination, the two spirits merged into one unfathomably powerful entity: the Lich King. From his citadel in Icecrown, he now commands the endless undead armies of the Scourge, threatening all of Azeroth.

Formerly champions of the Scourge, the Death Knights of Acherus broke free from the Lich King's control following the battle of Light's Hope Chapel. Now led by Highlord Darion Mograine, the Knights of the Ebon Blade have sworn a singular oath: to see the Lich King destroyed, no matter the cost.
